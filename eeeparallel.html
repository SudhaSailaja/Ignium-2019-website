Rules



ELIGIBILITY:

The event is open to all regular B.Tech students


EVENT DESCRIPTION:
Round 1:
Participant are supposed to answer to the questions related to missing letters, jumbled words and pictowords, all the word being technical terms.

Round 2:
Students are given the audio of an advertisement and they have to guess the product and also company's nameto the given product.

Round 3:
In this round, students are asked to solve MCQs(not the authentic options)with questions of varying complexities.

Round 4:
The goal is to find out a word with the help of three clues whuch are to be distributed among three different teams. Each chit given to a team contains two clues. the first one would pair themselves with team having hte clue for the same word. In the end all the three teams of the same group have to find each other and join all the clues tpgether forming the ultimate word.

Round 5: Participants are provided with the elements related to which they have solved the riddles and are asked to design a circiut to obtain the desired output from a given input.
