Sherlock Holmes



Attention to all the Sherlock fans out there! IGNIUM 2K18 brings to you a head-scratcher of a crime scene to solve where you assume the role of a real investigator. Bring out the Sherlock Holmes in you to find the murderer before he finds you.

Rules



ELIGIBILITY:

The event is open to all B.E/B.TECH/M.TECH students.

EVENT DESCRIPTION:
Round 1: Situations involving a crime scene will be given, they need to put themselves in the shoes of the murderer and figure out the way to escape from the murder. Judgement will be done based on how creatively they escape from the murder.
Round 2: Contestants will be experienced with a real crime scene, they need to interact with the characters to solve the given crime case.
Round 3: Contestants need to give a detailed description of their investigation and need to write the entire plot based on their investigation.
GUIDELINES:
  • This event is open for all the departments.
  • There is a time limit of maximum 1 hour 30 minutes for all participants to complete the event.
  • Judging is completely based on how creatively you solve the case.
  • Round 1 is an elimination round.
