Jittery Trigger



Can you recognize logos and images in a jiffy? IGNIUM 2K18 presents to you the ultra fun-filled event, Jittery trigger. This is an event, which triggers your creativity and presence of mind while testing your GK and spontaneity.

Rules



ELIGIBILITY:

The event is open to all.

EVENT DESCRIPTION:
Round 1:
  • Level 1:  Technical picture guess with a mix of rebuses or logical quest-A few pictures will be shown as slide show, participants has to relate correct technical/non technical word related to those pictures.
  • Level 2:  Guessing company names-The slogans/ taglines for companies will be given, participants have to recognize the name of company.
Round 2:
  • Level 1:  A grid will be given with randomly arranged letters. 15 questions will be given based on general stuff( tech/non tech). The participants need to write the answers against the question and also mark it in the grid.
  • Level 2:  A word will be given and participants are expected to make many words from that wordâ€™s letters.
Round 3:
  • Level 1:  Easy and answerable questions will be asked. The participant has to give the wrong answer. Within a time limit, points will be given based on how many answers they have answered incorrectly.
  • Level 2:  Tic Tac Toe: 9 topics are placed in 9 places .So if the team wants to make a mark , they need to choose a topic & answer the question related to selected topic.
GUIDELINES:
  • This event will take place in three rounds.
