Manetronics



A wholesome event that touches upon all aspects an engineer is required to excel at. It gauges the readiness of an Electronics engineer to face the competitive world out there by putting the participants through three rigorous rounds of Aptitude, Technical and Corporate tests.

Rules



ELIGIBILITY :


The event is open to all regular B.Tech students


EVENT DESCRIPTION:
Round 1 :
An Aptitude Test consisting of Quantitative ability, Logical Reasoning and Technical based questions.

Round 2 :
This is a managerial round to test one's creative, marketing and team coordination abilities.

Round 3 :
The final round tests one's knowledge and proficiency in the ECE domain through an electronic hunt for circuit connections and components.
